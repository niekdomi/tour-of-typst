# Lists

Every experiment needs a materials list and a procedure. Ours involves bread, butter, a high-speed
camera, and a worrying disregard for kitchen hygiene. Typst has two types of lists:

**Unordered lists** use `-`:

```typst
- White sandwich bread
- Salted butter
  - Spreadable, applied to one side only
```

**Numbered lists** use `+`:

```typst
+ Prepare one slice with butter on top
+ Hold at table height
+ Release without spin
+ Record the fall
+ Weep at the result
```

> [!TIP] Indent with two spaces to create nested lists. This is how we specify sub-items like bread
> thickness and butter brand.
